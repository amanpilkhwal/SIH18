Solution For Plain Terrain


  • Sewage and wastewater drainage into traditional water bodies should be avoided and hence Government intervention at the source is urgently required

  • Trees (requiring less water and providing with more oxygen) should be planted within 100m range of lakes, streams and rivers.

  • Trees like Ficus religiosa(pipal), Ficus racemosa(guular) can be planted.

  • Ground drilling should be done for the extraction of water.
